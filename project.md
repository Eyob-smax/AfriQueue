## Platform Overview

ArifQueue solves the critical problem of long waiting times at healthcare facilities by providing:

- **Real-time queue visibility** — Patients can see their position and estimated wait time
- **Smart clinic discovery** — Find nearby clinics based on location, services, and current wait times
- **AI-powered health guidance** — Get preliminary symptom assessment before visiting a clinic
- **Seamless communication** — Direct messaging between patients, staff, and administrators
- **Operational insights** — Clinics get data-driven insights to improve patient flow

The platform serves seven African countries: Ethiopia, Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Tanzania, and Uganda.

---

## User Roles

### Patients (Clients)
Individuals seeking healthcare services who want to reduce waiting time and find the right clinic for their needs.

### Clinic Staff
Healthcare workers who manage queues, serve patients, and communicate with clients at their assigned health center.

### Platform Administrators
System managers who approve new clinics and staff, monitor platform health, and ensure quality control.

## Patient Features

### 1. Registration & Onboarding

**Multiple Sign-Up Options:**
- Email and password
- Google account (OAuth)
- Phone number with SMS verification (OTP)

**Location-Based Setup:**
- Select your country from seven supported nations
- Choose your city from a comprehensive list
- System automatically shows clinics in your area

**Health Profile (Optional):**
- Current health conditions
- Medical history
- Chronic illnesses
- Blood type
- Emergency contact information
- Accessibility needs or disabilities

### 2. Clinic Discovery

**Find Clinics Near You:**
- Interactive map showing all health centers in your city
- Filter by distance from your current location
- View clinic details: address, services, specialties, operating hours
- See real-time queue status for each clinic

**Smart Search:**
- Clinics are automatically filtered by your country and city
- Distance calculation shows nearest facilities first
- View available services and specialties before visiting

### 3. Queue Management

**Join a Queue:**
- Browse active queues at any clinic in your area
- See current queue length and estimated wait time
- Select the service type you need (e.g., General Consultation, Lab Tests, Vaccination)
- Receive a queue number instantly

**Real-Time Updates:**
- Track your position in the queue live
- Get notified when your turn is approaching
- See how many people are ahead of you
- Receive alerts if the queue is paused or delayed

**Queue Control:**
- Leave a queue if plans change
- View your queue history
- See completed appointments
- Track all active reservations across multiple clinics

### 4. AI Symptom Checker

**Health Assistant:**
- Chat with an AI-powered health assistant
- Describe your symptoms in natural language
- Get preliminary health guidance and triage
- Receive recommendations for appropriate care level
- Educational information about common conditions

**Smart Recommendations:**
- AI suggests whether you need urgent care, general consultation, or specialist
- Helps you understand when to seek immediate medical attention
- Provides health tips and self-care guidance
- Matches symptoms to appropriate clinic services

**Privacy-First:**
- Conversations are for guidance only, not diagnosis
- Clear disclaimers about AI limitations
- Encourages professional medical consultation

### 5. Appointments & History

**Track Your Healthcare:**
- View all past queue reservations
- See completed appointments with dates and services
- Access your healthcare timeline
- Review which clinics you've visited

### 6. Profile Management

**Update Your Information:**
- Edit personal details (name, phone, email)
- Update location (country and city)
- Modify health profile information
- Change emergency contacts
- Update accessibility needs

## Clinic Staff Features

### 1. Staff Registration & Approval

**Apply to Join:**
- Register with email, phone, or Google account
- Specify which health center you work at
- Provide professional details (license number, department, role)
- Submit application for admin review

**Approval Process:**
- Application goes to platform administrators
- Admin reviews credentials and health center information
- Staff receives email notification of approval or rejection
- Upon approval, full access to staff dashboard is granted

### 2. Queue Board (Live Management)

**Real-Time Queue View:**
- See all patients currently in queue
- View queue numbers, patient names, and wait times
- Monitor multiple queues for different services
- Track queue status (active, paused, closed)

**Patient Management:**
- Advance queue when patient is served
- Mark reservations as completed
- Cancel no-shows or invalid reservations
- View patient contact information for follow-up

**Queue Actions:**
- Call next patient in line
- Skip or reorder if needed
- Pause queue during breaks
- Close queue when capacity is reached

### 3. Queue Creation & Management

**Create New Queues:**
- Set up queues for specific services (e.g., Vaccination, Lab Tests, General Consultation)
- Define queue date and time
- Set maximum capacity
- Choose queue status (active, paused, closed)

**Edit Existing Queues:**
- Update service type
- Modify capacity limits
- Change queue status
- Adjust date and time

**Multi-Service Support:**
- Run multiple queues simultaneously for different services
- Separate queues for different departments
- Manage walk-ins vs. scheduled appointments

### 4. Clinic Insights & Analytics

**Performance Metrics:**
- **Average Wait Time:** See how long patients wait on average
- **Trend Analysis:** Compare today's wait time vs. yesterday (percentage change)
- **Patients Seen Today:** Track daily patient volume
- **Volume Trends:** Compare today's patient count vs. yesterday

**Peak Traffic Analysis:**
- View busiest hours of the day
- Identify peak traffic patterns (8 AM, 10 AM, 12 PM, 2 PM, 4 PM, 6 PM)
- Plan staffing based on historical data
- Optimize queue management during rush hours

**Patient Logs:**
- View recent patient visits
- See patient names, services, and dates
- Track reservation status (pending, completed, cancelled)
- Access patient history for follow-up

### 5. Health Center Settings

**Clinic Information:**
- View assigned health center details
- See clinic location and contact information
- Access clinic services and specialties
- Check operating status

### 6. Direct Patient Communication

**Messaging:**
- Chat directly with patients who have visited your clinic
- Answer questions about services
- Provide appointment reminders
- Send follow-up messages

## User Journeys

### Patient Journey: Finding Care

1. **Sign Up** — Register with email, phone, or Google
2. **Set Location** — Choose your country and city
3. **Complete Profile** — Add health information (optional)
4. **Check Symptoms** — Use AI assistant for guidance (optional)
5. **Find Clinics** — Browse nearby health centers on map
6. **Join Queue** — Select a clinic and service, get queue number
7. **Track Position** — Monitor your place in line in real-time
8. **Get Notified** — Receive alert when your turn approaches
9. **Visit Clinic** — Arrive at the right time, skip the wait
10. **Follow Up** — Message staff if needed, view history

### Staff Journey: Managing Queues

1. **Apply** — Register and submit staff application
2. **Get Approved** — Admin reviews and approves application
3. **Access Dashboard** — Log in to staff queue board
4. **Create Queue** — Set up queue for today's service
5. **Monitor Patients** — See who's in line in real-time
6. **Serve Patients** — Advance queue as each patient is seen
7. **View Insights** — Check wait times and patient volume
8. **Communicate** — Message patients for follow-up
9. **Manage Flow** — Pause or close queue as needed
10. **Review Logs** — Access patient history and analytics

### Admin Journey: Platform Management

1. **Log In** — Access admin dashboard
2. **Review Overview** — See platform statistics
3. **Check Requests** — View pending staff applications
4. **Approve Staff** — Review credentials and approve qualified applicants
5. **Manage Centers** — Create or edit health center information
6. **Monitor Activity** — Check queue reports and system health
7. **Handle Issues** — Suspend problematic accounts if needed
8. **Review Audit** — Check logs of all administrative actions
9. **Communicate** — Message staff or other admins
10. **Ensure Quality** — Maintain platform integrity and user experience
